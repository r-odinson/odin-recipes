Sizzling Tofu

Description

This Filipino version of stir-fried tofu is a guaranteed crowd pleaser. It can stand out as a main course or as an appetizer. Often served on a sizzling plate in restaurants, this vegetarian friendly sisig will always want you craving more.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoon oil, 1 T for pan-frying tofu, 1 T for sauteeing garlic and onion
  • 2 tablespoons garlic, chopped
  • 1/2 cup white onione, chopped
  • 750 grams firm tofu, cubed
  • 2 tablespoons liquid seasoning
  • 1/2 teaspoon hot sauce
  • 1/4 cup bell pepper, red and or green, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoon red chili, chopped
  • 1/4 cup mayo
  • 1/2 tablespoon margarine

Steps

  1. Prepare tofu - Drain tofu and pat dry with paper towl. Slice tofu into half-inch cubes but feel free to adjust according to preference.
  2. Fry tofu - Heat 1 tablespoon oil in a pan over medium to high heat. Add cubed tofu. Pan fry until cubes are golden brown and set aside.
  3. Sauté and combine other ingredients - Sauté garlic, white onion, bell pepper, then chili. Lower heat and add tofu cubes. Pour in the liquid seasoning, soy sauce, and hot sauce. Mix well until tofu cubes are evenly coated. Remove pan from heat.
  4. Serving and plating - Add mayo and margarine into cooled down pan and toss mixture well. Do this right before serving to keep it creamy and prevent the mayo and margarine from completely melting. Transfer to a heated sizzling plate or serving bowl of choice. Best served and enjoyed while warm.
