Jasmine Green Tea

Description

Achieving balance in this tea is what makes it really shine. By bringing together different expressions of jasmine - as fragrant flowers, also naturally infused into rolled green tea leaves - this tea maintains its gentle nature while being strong enough to stand alone and stand out. After all, there is a reason Uncle Iroh named his shop after it! It is a remindender of the very nature of life: Where there is balance, there is peace.

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup hot water
  • 1 teaspoon dried jasmine flowers
  • 1/2 teaspoon Japanese sencha
  • 2 rolled jasmine pears

Steps

  1. Boil water to 180 ℉ or about 80 °C.
  2. Place jasmine flowers, sencha, and jasmine pearls into a small teapot.
  3. Discard first steep and add fresh hot water. Let steep 3 minutes, then serve immediately.
